What We Do

Our Fabrication & Erection Services

We combine high-grade materials, precision engineering, and safe erection practices to build structures that stand the test of time.

Heavy Steel Erection

Professional erection of massive columns, overhead cranes, industrial gantries, and heavy-duty steel trusses using advanced lifting methodologies.

  • High-Load Column Placement
  • Structural Alignment & Bolting
  • Industrial Cranes & Gantries

Industrial Sheds & Roofing

Design, fabrication, and complete site erection of large-span industrial sheds, warehouses, factories, and commercial steel roofing complexes.

  • Large-Span Roof Trusses
  • Warehouse Frame Assembly
  • Polycarbonate & Metal Cladding

Custom Iron & Steel Works

Bespoke fabrication of metal platforms, walkways, industrial staircases, fire escapes, heavy-duty safety gates, and boundary grills.

  • Mezzanine Floors & Platforms
  • Heavy-Duty Grills & Gates
  • Access Staircases & Ladders

Maintenance & Reinforcement

Structural health inspection, reinforcement of weak joints, extension of existing sheds, and modifications of existing heavy steel frames.

  • Joint Strengthening & Welding
  • Structure Extension Work
  • Rust Mitigation & Recoating
